# Google Ads Campaign Brief: SpookyEmail

## Product Overview

**URL:** https://spookyemail.com

**What it does:** AI-powered tool that generates personalized email sequences for outbound sales. Users upload a CSV of contacts, write a prompt template, and the tool generates unique emails for each prospect using Google Gemini AI.

**Pricing:**
- Free with your own API key
- $0.05 per email using our API key

## Target Audience

### Primary
- B2B sales professionals doing cold outreach
- SDRs (Sales Development Reps)
- Founders/CEOs doing their own outbound
- Small sales teams (1-10 people)

### Secondary
- Marketing agencies managing outbound for clients
- Recruiters doing candidate outreach
- Business development professionals

### Firmographics
- Company size: 1-200 employees
- Industries: SaaS, professional services, agencies, recruiting
- Geography: US, UK, Canada, Australia (English-speaking)
